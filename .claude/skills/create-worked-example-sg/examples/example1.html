Balancing the Equation

Big Idea: We can find the weight of an unknown object using a hanger diagram. Just like a balanced scale, if you add or remove the same amount from both sides, it stays equal!

The RPG Crafting Station

Step 1: Simplify

Check for Understanding: Why did I choose to cross out exactly one Crystal from both sides?

Step 1: Simplify

To remove the duplicates! By removing the crystal on the right, I get closer to isolating the unknown variables.

Step 2: Isolate

Check for Understanding: How did I decide to remove 2 Gold Bars from both sides?

Step 2: Isolate

I needed the Crystals alone on the left. Since 2 Gold Bars were "cluttering" the left side, I had to remove them (and do the same to the right)!

The Final Solution

We removed duplicates and isolated the variable.

2 Crystals = 4 Gold Bars

Therefore, if we split both piles in half...

1 Crystal = 2 Gold Bars

By keeping the "hanger" balanced at every step, we found the true value of the item.
