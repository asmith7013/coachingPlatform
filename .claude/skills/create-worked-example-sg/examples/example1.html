Balance and Isolate

Unit 4 Lesson 1: Solving Equations with Hanger Diagrams

Today's Strategy

To find an unknown value, we BALANCE by removing equal amounts from both sides, then ISOLATE the variable to find its value.

The Steps:

  1. BALANCE - Remove matching items from both sides
  2. ISOLATE - Get the unknown alone on one side
  3. SOLVE - Divide to find the value of one

Learning Goal:

Students will be able to solve equations using hanger diagrams by balancing and isolating variables.
