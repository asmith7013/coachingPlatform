STEP 1: DIVIDE BY 3

3(x - 2) = 8

3(x - 2) ÷ 3

=

8 ÷ 3

↓ The 3's cancel on the left side

x - 2 = 8/3

✅ ANSWER
Because the 3 on the outside is MULTIPLYING the entire quantity (x - 2). When we divide by 3, we're undoing that multiplication, so the 3's cancel out completely! This is why dividing first is so powerful—it instantly removes the coefficient.
