Crosses y-axis at (0, 0)

Jordan's Playlist

y = 3x + 5

Crosses y-axis at (0, 5)

DIFFERENT Y-INTERCEPT = VERTICAL SHIFT

Jordan's line is shifted UP by 5 units

Every point on Jordan's line is 5 units higher!

✅ ANSWER
The y-intercept tells us where each line STARTS. Different y-intercepts mean one line is shifted vertically from the other!
