Step 3: Compare

What's the SAME?

  • Both have slope = 3
  • Same steepness (change in y: 3, change in x: 1)
  • Lines are parallel
  • Same rate of change

What's DIFFERENT?

  • y-intercepts: 0 vs 5
  • Different starting points
  • Jordan's line is shifted up 5
  • Different initial values

Complete Answer

The graphs are THE SAME because they have the same slope (3), making them parallel with identical steepness.

They are DIFFERENT because y = 3x + 5 has a y-intercept of 5 instead of 0, shifting the entire line up 5 units.
