Learning Goal: Create an equation in one variable to represent when two conditions are equal. Interpret the solution in context.

Strategy: Model, Match, Meaning - MODEL each situation with an expression, MATCH by setting them equal, explain the MEANING of the solution.

Problem 1: Food Delivery Battle

Two food delivery apps are competing for your business. Let d = number of deliveries.

DashDine

$10 monthly membership

+ $2 delivery fee per order

QuickBite

No membership fee

$4 delivery fee per order

Your Task:

1. Write an expression for each app's total cost.
2. Write and solve an equation to find when they cost the same.
3. Explain what the solution means in context.
