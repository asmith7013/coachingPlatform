y = 3x and y = 3x - 4: Same and Different

THE SAME:

  • Both have slope = 3
  • Same steepness/rate of change
  • Both are parallel lines

DIFFERENT:

  • Y-intercepts: 0 vs -4
  • Different starting points
  • y = 3x - 4 is shifted down 4 units

ANSWER

ViewMax started 4 million subscribers behind. StreamFlix started at 0. Both grow at 3 million subscribers per month, so they stay the same distance apart forever!
