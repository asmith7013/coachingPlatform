Elevation Hike

Apply the Identify and Compare strategy

Two hikers are climbing a mountain at the same rate but started at different elevations:

Hiker A: y = 2x + 3
Hiker B: y = 2x - 4

where x = hours hiking and y = elevation in hundreds of feet

Your Task: Describe how the graph of y = 2x + 3 is the same and different from the graph of y = 2x - 4.
