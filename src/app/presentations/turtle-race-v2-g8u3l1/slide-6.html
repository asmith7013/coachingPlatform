Step 2: Calculate Half the Speed

"Half as fast" means dividing the speed by 2.

Turtle g's speed:

2 cm/sec

Half as fast:

2 / 2 = 1 cm/sec

Turtle g

2 cm/s

New turtle

1 cm/s

Half the height = Half the speed

ANSWER
1 cm/sec. We divide by 2 because "half as fast" means the new turtle covers half the distance in the same time.
