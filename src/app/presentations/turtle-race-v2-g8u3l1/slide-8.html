Step 3: Explain Your Reasoning

How Line h Shows "Half as Fast"

1. Less Steep Slope

Line h has half the slope of line g. A gentler slope means slower speed.

2. Half the Distance

In the same amount of time (6 sec), turtle h travels only 6 cm while turtle g travels 12 cm.

3. Half the Constant of Proportionality

The equation d = 1t represents turtle h, where 1 is exactly half of turtle g's constant (2).

Key Insight: Slope = Speed = Constant of Proportionality
