Step 2: Find the Slope (Rate of Change)

The slope tells us how much y changes for every 1 unit change in x. Use any two points from the table.

x y
0 0
3 6
5 10
6 12

Using points (0, 0) and (3, 6):

slope = change in y / change in x

= (6 - 0) / (3 - 0) = 6 / 3 = 2
